How it works

The same method used by Caroline's private students

1
Listen. Caroline records each expression twice — once at natural speed, once slowly. Your ear adjusts before your mouth tries.
2
Record yourself. Press the button, say the expression, listen back. Hearing yourself is where the real progress happens.
3
Test your memory. A quiz every 5 expressions keeps you honest. The final quiz shows you exactly what sticks.
