What is I Can Speak French?

I Can Speak French is a monthly audio training subscription by SnapEnglish for English speakers who want to speak natural, everyday French. Each month: 25 authentic French expressions to listen to, record in your own voice, and play back — the Echo Bounce loop — until they come out naturally.

Most French you've learned sounds like French — but not like the French people actually speak. "Je voudrais un café" gets the point across; "Un café, s'il vous plaît" is what you say at a counter. The gap between textbook French and natural French isn't vocabulary — it's the expressions, the rhythm, and the sounds you've never been asked to produce yourself.

That's what I Can Speak French trains. One month at a time, one expression at a time.

The method

Echo Bounce — listen, record, play back

Created by Caroline Cousin and used in Sound Less French (the mirror product for French speakers learning English), Echo Bounce is built on one insight: you can't hear your own accent while you're speaking. Your brain is too busy building the sentence. The moment you play back a recording, it becomes an listener — and it hears everything.

1
Listen

Hear an authentic French expression recorded by a native speaker — with its natural rhythm, intonation, and connected speech. Not isolated words: a full phrase, the way it's said in real life.

2
Record yourself

Say it out loud and record. Short phrase, full attention. This is the step most learners skip — and the one that makes all the difference.

3
Play back and compare

Listen to yourself, then re-listen to the native model. You'll hear the gap without anyone pointing it out — and that self-diagnosed correction is what makes it stick. Repeat until the gap closes.

Twenty minutes of Echo Bounce is worth hours of passive listening. Listening trains your comprehension. Echo Bounce trains your mouth.
